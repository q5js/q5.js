## What is q5?

q5.js WebGPU is the fastest, most beginner friendly 2D graphics library on the web!

It was designed to make creative coding fun and accessible for educators, designers, artists, and beginners.

# 5-0

## Addons

# 5-1

q5 works with popular addons.

# 5-2

[p5.sound](https://p5js.org/reference/p5.sound/) adds audio generation, signal analysis, and advanced mixing capabilities. [See p5.sound demos.](https://openprocessing.org/curation/88608)

# 5-3

[q5play](https://q5play.org) adds high quality Box2D physics simulation, better input handling, a camera, and animations.

# 5-4

[ml5.js](https://ml5js.org/) adds AI capabilities like hand tracking, face detection, and body pose estimation. [See ml5 demos.](https://openprocessing.org/curation/88587)
